Staking tutorial

Learn how to stake your Home Coins to earn up to 18.32% in rewards.

 

1. Connect your wallet

Before you get starter, please prepare a Metamask wallet (RuufPay currently only supports Metamask).

On the homepage, click "Connect Wallet".

 

Connect your wallet to RuufPay in Metamask.

 

After the wallet is connected, you will see your wallet address on the top

 

2. Stake your coins

After connecting with Metamask, you will see the number of Home Coins you have in your wallet

*** If you don't own any Home Coin you can buy them in Uniswap or Coinsbit ***

To stake your Home Coins enter the number of coins you want to stake or click the Max button stake all your coins.

 

After selecting the desired amount, click on the "STAKE" button and confirm the popup clicking "YES" to start the staking.

 

A Metamask window will show with the transaction data. Confirm it and wait until the transaction is validated.

 

After that your Home Coins are staked properly.

 

 

Are you ready?

Go to the staking page and start staking now
